Entrepreneurial spirit meets established culture
Intercope was founded by an IT pioneering entrepreneur. That entrepreneurial culture and spirit prevail today, allowing our employees to innovate ideas with the support of a flat hierarchical team structure. We have kept the belief of an open and flexible work environment going; for over 30 years.

We invest in you
We enjoy having our employees around. And that feeling is mutual. So much so, that most employees stay with us for many years. That can only be achieved by forming long-term commitments and the promise to support you in your professional progression.

Flexibility is our mantra
If it comes to working hours, location or job progression, we truly believe in flexibility and that nothing is set in stone. We all know that life keeps changing and your job at Intercope can do the same. We would like to be part of your journey.

Think outside the BOX
To be a leader, we need people to think outside the box. Our employees create unique and innovative software that keeps a vital part of our customer’s business safe. Joining Intercope means to think ahead and bring your ideas to life.
